# E-commerce Revenue Analysis Project

## Project Overview
The goal was to understand **what factors are influencing overall revenue performance** 

The project focuses on:

- Business understanding and KPI analysis  
- Identifying repeat customers and revenue drivers  
- Investigating product performance and returns  
- Analyzing city-level and marketing channel impacts  
- Data storytelling to provide actionable recommendations  

---

## Business Problem
Management wanted to answer:

> What factors are influencing overall revenue performance?

---

## Key Findings
1. **Top Revenue Category:** Beauty generates the highest revenue.  
2. **High-Performing City:** Multan contributes the most revenue.  
3. **Average Order Value (AOV):** $13.76 per product.  
4. **Repeat Customers:** 133 customers purchase multiple products repeatedly.  
5. **Effective Marketing Channel:** Instagram drives the highest revenue.  
6. **Problematic Product:** Jeans have the highest return rate (~36%), impacting profitability.

---

## Recommendations
1. Focus marketing efforts on high-revenue **Beauty products**.  
2. Investigate lower-performing cities like **Lahore** to resolve product or service issues.  
3. Retain repeat customers by improving **customer service** and providing **reliable products**.  
4. Improve quality and sizing of **Jeans** to reduce returns and increase revenue.

---

## Analysis Approach
1. **Business Understanding:** Identified revenue drivers and key KPIs.  
2. **KPI Analysis:** Revenue, units sold, average order value, return rates, and repeat customers.  
3. **Exploratory Data Analysis:** Examined trends by category, product, city, and marketing channel.  
4. **Return Analysis:** Highlighted high-return products and categories.  
5. **Storytelling:** Combined findings into clear business insights with actionable recommendations.
